Warranty and Returns

Warranty

  • Standard term: 14 calendar days from the date of receiving the product.
  • Conditions: intact packaging, no installation traces, and preserved manufacturer's markings.
  • Warranty case: factory defect or non-compliance with specifications.
  1. Contact us via phone or email.
  2. Pack and send the product to our service center.
  3. We will replace the product or refund the money within 7 days after defect confirmation.

Returns

  • Return of proper quality products: within 14 days from the date of purchase.
  • Return of defective products: without time limitation, with reimbursement of the cost and delivery.
  1. Provide the order number and the reason for return.
  2. Pack the part in the original packaging.
  3. Send it via "Nova Poshta" to our address with a tracking number (TTN).
  4. Refund within 14 days after inspection.

Exceptions

  • Non-returnable: parts with installation traces or returns without prior agreement.
  • Some products may have individual conditions specified in the description.
