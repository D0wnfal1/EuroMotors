Delivery and Payment

Delivery

Delivery Times

  • Within the same city: up to 1 business day
  • Between regional centers or large cities: 1–2 business days
  • To remote settlements: up to 3 business days

Storage and Tracking

After arrival, the parcel is stored at the branch free of charge for 7 days. From the eighth day onwards — according to Nova Poshta's tariffs.

You will receive a tracking number (TTN) for online tracking in the mobile app or on the website.

Payment

Prepayment via LiqPay

  1. Select LiqPay in the cart and proceed to the payment form.
  2. Enter your card details and confirm the payment.
  3. A commission fee of 1.5% of the payment amount applies. Funds are transferred online immediately after confirmation.
  4. The payment is secured and activated instantly after confirmation.

Payment Upon Receipt (Cash on Delivery)

Pay for your order at the branch when receiving the parcel.

  • Commission fee — 2% of the amount + 20 ₴.
  • Cash on delivery allows you to inspect the goods before payment.
  • No charges apply if you refuse to accept the parcel.
